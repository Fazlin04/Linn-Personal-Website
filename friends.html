My Friends

Being an introverted person means I have a small circle of friends. From high school to university, I’ve always stuck with a few close friends whom I feel comfortable with.

So, let me introduce you to my friends from my high school days and university life!


My High School Friends :

This is a picture of my high school friends. Starting from the left, we have Siti Nur Balqis, whom we call Balqis. We’ve been friends since we were 14 years old (Form 1). At first, she seemed hard to approach, but I didn’t expect her to be such a bright and cheerful person. Interestingly, she continued her studies at the same place as me, UiTM Sungai Petani, though we are in different courses. She is pursuing a banking course.

In the middle is Nur Nabila, whom we affectionately call Bella. She is incredibly smart in accounting, and I’ve always looked up to her for guidance in the subject. We’ve known each other since primary school. Bella is a very shy person, but she is very kind. Currently, she is studying accounting at UiTM Tapah.

Lastly, on the right, is Nur Sakinah, whom we call Kinah. She’s a math genius, and since I’m not great at math, I often asked her for help. She was always patient and willing to guide me, no matter how long it took for me to understand. Now, she’s studying accounting at Politeknik Kota Bharu and is doing her internship in her final semester.

It has been such a long time since we last met, and I really miss them. However, with everyone leading busy lives and living in different states, meeting up has become difficult. Despite that, I’m incredibly grateful that we still keep in touch. I feel blessed to have such amazing and supportive high school friends.
